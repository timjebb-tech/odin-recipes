Tim's Beans on toast

Description

This is the only way to make beans on toast

Ingredients

  • 210g Aldi or Branston backed beans
  • Large slice of Aldi seeded sourdough bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Toast the bread until it starts to smoke.
  2. Microwave the beans for 90 seconds (900 watt microwave)
  3. Thickly butter the toast.
  4. Pour the beans on to the toast
