Tomato soup

Description

How to make tomato soup

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tomato soup

Steps

  1. Open the can
  2. Pour the contents into a microwaveable bowl. Do not cover
  3. Microwave for 90 seconds (900 w microwave)
  4. Stir
  5. Microwave for a further 60 seconds (900 w microwave)
  6. Stir
  7. Pour into a bowl
